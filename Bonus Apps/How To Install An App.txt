How To Install An App In Hash Window.
1. Drag Your Desired Installer To The Folder Where Your hash_Window.py file Is.
2. Open A Terminal
3. Go To Your hash Window Directory.
4. On Windows, Type In: 'python "<name_of_installer>.py"' On Linux, Type In: 'python3 "<name_of_installer>.py"'
5. Follow The Instructions On Screen.
